How it works

From calendar to brief
in under 3 minutes

Morning Brief runs automatically every morning. By the time you open your laptop, your prep is done.

📅

Pulls your calendar

Connects to Google Calendar via OAuth. Reads today's events and identifies every external attendee (anyone outside your company).

🔍

Researches each person

Looks up the company, the person's role and background, recent news, funding stage, and GTM motion — in parallel for every meeting.

🎯

Tailors the pitch

Maps your product's use cases to each attendee's specific role. A CMO sees messaging research. A Head of Product sees concept testing. A CEO sees buyer persona work.

📄

Generates sample artifacts

Creates 1–2 realistic sample output files per meeting — showing what your product would actually produce for that company. Conversation starters, not pitch decks.

✨

Opens in your browser

A beautiful HTML brief appears at 7:30 AM every weekday. No app to open, no dashboard to log into. Just a page ready for you.

⚙️

Configured for your product

One config file drives everything — your company, use cases, target personas, competitors, and what types of sample outputs to generate.

Why it's different

Built for how sales actually works

🔑

Your keys, your data

Runs entirely on your machine with your own Anthropic API key and your own Google Calendar OAuth. No server ever sees your calendar or your meetings.

⚡

Actually automated

A launchd job (macOS) or cron job fires every weekday morning. You never have to remember to run it — it's just there when you wake up.

🎨

Fully customizable

Edit one config file to change your company info, use cases, and sample study types. Edit the slash command to change the brief design or workflow.

🤖

AI-moderated research

Powered by Claude — researches companies and people with real web searches, not cached or stale data. Every brief is based on what's true today.

📊

Sample artifacts that close

Instead of explaining what your product does, show a prospect a version built for their company. Tangible outputs beat abstract pitches every time.

🛠

No SaaS, no subscriptions

Pure open source. Clone it, configure it, run it. You only pay for what you use via your existing Anthropic account (typically cents per morning brief).

Get started

Set up in 5 minutes

Works on macOS. Linux support via cron.

🔒

You're never charged for anyone else's usage

Claude Code is installed per-user with your own Anthropic account. Morning Brief runs on your machine and bills to your account only. Everyone who sets this up uses their own API key — there's no shared infrastructure, no SaaS layer, and no way for one person's usage to affect another's.

1

Install Claude Code

Claude Code is Anthropic's AI coding CLI. Install it and authenticate with your own Anthropic account.

npm install -g @anthropic-ai/claude-code claude # authenticate with your Anthropic account
2

Connect your Google Calendar

Install the Google Calendar MCP connector. It authenticates via OAuth — your calendar, your credentials.

# Follow setup at: github.com/nspect/google-calendar-mcp
3

Configure your company

Copy the example config and fill in your product, use cases, and target personas.

cp config/company.example.md ~/.claude/morning-brief-config.md # Edit ~/.claude/morning-brief-config.md with your company info
4

Install the command

mkdir -p ~/.claude/commands cp commands/morning-brief.md ~/.claude/commands/morning-brief.md
5

Automate it

One command sets up the launchd job that runs every weekday at 7:30 AM.

bash automation/install-macos.sh

Or test it manually first: open Claude Code and type /morning-brief
